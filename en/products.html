Orthopedic Casting Tape

Synthetic Orthopedic Bandage

Synthetic orthopedic bandage manufactured from a fiberglass substrate impregnated with activated polyurethane resin. The fabric is stretchable in all directions, making it very comfortable to use and easy to mold.

Key Features:

  • Air permeable
  • Superior adhesion
  • Excellent molding
  • Moisture resistant
  • X-ray transparent
  • Lightweight and robust

Size and Color Chart:

Width Length Colors
2" (5.0 cm) 3.6 m White, Blue, Pink, Orange, Green, Purple
3" (7.5 cm) 3.6 m White, Blue, Pink, Orange, Green
4" (10.0 cm) 3.6 m White, Blue, Pink, Purple
5" (12.5 cm) 3.6 m White, Blue, Green

PERFORMANCE

4 Min. Setting
20 Min. Load

Instructions for Use

  1. 1 Wear medical gloves.
  2. 2 Immerse in water (20-25°C) for 10 sec.
  3. 3 Apply in spiral (2-3 layers max).
  4. 4 Mold and let dry for 5 min.

JERSEY FABRIC

Antibacterial Cotton Protection

Jersey Fabric is the first layer applied when placing a medical cast. Designed to evacuate perspiration, it protects the skin under resins, splints and synthetic bandages.
